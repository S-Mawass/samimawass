Turning data into clarity, strategy, and action.

A Lebanese Baccalaureate in General Science (2018) and a BSc in Management Information Systems (Lebanese University, 2022) gave me the analytical rigour and systems thinking to move directly into data and analytics.

Career path.

I started through an internship in 2021 and moved into a full-time role in 2022 as a BI Developer and Analytics Engineer at Cedars BC. Since then I've delivered 18+ end-to-end engagements across government, higher education, FMCG, and energy — helping organisations turn opaque reporting into semantic models stakeholders trust. My work spans the GCC and Europe across 7 countries.

How I work.

What motivates me most is solving business problems. I translate complex requirements into practical solutions, uncover insights that support decision-making, and design analytics experiences that create measurable value. My approach combines technical depth with business understanding — bridging the gap between stakeholders, processes, and technology.

Most reporting fails not because the data is wrong, but because the model is opaque — unclear grain, unnamed measures, no documentation. I build semantic layers a stakeholder can actually follow.

Recently I re-architected a UAE higher-education institution's SSAS Tabular model into a clean star schema, cutting dataset refresh from ~30 minutes to ~6 — and trained a business user to own row-level security independently.

Performance highlight

Re-architected an SSAS Tabular model into a star schema for a UAE higher-education institution (~25k students) — cutting dataset refresh from ~30 min to ~6 min (~80% reduction) and enabling a clean production RLS hand-off to the client team.

Looking ahead.

I continue to expand my knowledge in modern analytics platforms, AI-enabled analytics, and data leadership. My goal is straightforward: help organisations make better decisions by turning data into clarity, strategy, and action.
