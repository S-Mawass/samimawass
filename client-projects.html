Higher Education · UAE · Jan 2025 to May 2026 · Featured

UAE Higher Education Analytics Programme

A suite of five production dashboards for a UAE national higher-education institution of roughly 25,700 students, built on a SQL Server to SSAS Tabular to Power BI stack and deployed to Power BI Report Server with production row-level security. I contributed as a BI Developer within the delivery team, owning SQL views in SSMS, SSAS Tabular model enhancements, Power BI report builds, and DAX. One key enhancement: I optimized the SSAS Tabular model behind the Education and Employment Observatory dashboard, which I joined mid-build, cutting dataset refresh from roughly 30 minutes to about 6 minutes, an improvement of around 80 percent.

Dashboards delivered:

  • Education & Employment Observatory
  • Apprenticeship Performance & Placement Analytics
  • Academic Resource Planning & Faculty Capacity
  • Student Directory & Demographics Intelligence
  • Institutional Sentiment & Digital Reputation

FMCG · Scotland · 2023 to 2024 · Featured

FMCG Manufacturing Analytics Suite

A SQL-backed Power BI suite for a leading Scottish food manufacturer, spanning five workstreams across three automated production lines. It includes live-connected OEE monitoring deployed on the production floor, multi-dimension waste analytics, planned versus unplanned downtime intelligence, real-time production-versus-target tracking, and a four-page financial reporting suite covering P&L, Cash Flow, and Balance Sheet. I owned the BI layer end to end, writing SQL views in SSMS, building the data models and dashboards, and running client requirements sessions, documentation, and handover training. The OEE breakdown identified speed loss as the primary efficiency driver, redirecting engineering attention.

Dashboards delivered:

  • OEE Live Production Dashboard
  • Waste Management & Quality Analysis
  • Downtime Analysis & Maintenance Intelligence
  • Production Statistics & Output Performance
  • Financial Reporting Suite (P&L, Cash Flow, Balance Sheet)
