About

I help teams trust their numbers.

Most reporting fails not because the data is wrong, but because the model is opaque — unclear grain, unnamed measures, no documentation. I build semantic layers a stakeholder can actually follow.

With 4+ years delivering BI across government, higher education, FMCG, and energy, I'm now extending that foundation into analytics engineering — dbt, modular SQL, and Azure pipelines.

Based in Lebanon, serving UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, and remote engagements across the GCC and Europe.
